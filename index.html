About Us

Restoration, Conservation and Protection

We are a non-governmental and a non-profit based organization of indigenous volunteers primarily concerned with the priorities regarding restoration, conservation and protection of lands, forests, vegetation, for communities that may have been affected by various mining operations in Sierra Leone.

  • Vision
  • Mission

We envision a society where mining should not be a factor for communities around to be affected by the ever-rising climate crises in the world, and to see that multinational corporations are held accountable to communities they operate.

Read More

We strive to ensure accountability by multinational corporations operating in Sierra Leone through advocacy, lobby, awareness and capacity building of affected communities, research and the restoration of lost lands and natural vegetation.
